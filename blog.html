The 7 Rhythms of Capoeira Regional
August 09, 2017

Capoeira music differentiates Capoeira from other martial arts. Music is a very important aspect in Capoeira. The musical variations of the berimbau are the various rhythms performed by the player to define the type of play that will be done on the "roda". A good capoeirista should, or rather, have an obligation, to know the greatest number of rhythms, as well as the meaning and type of game practiced in each of these rhythms.
